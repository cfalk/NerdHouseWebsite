As inhabitants of the Yarnall Community House, we hope to create a space that caters to the needs of Haverford's categorically diverse nerd population. In creating a space where gaming, tech, TV, fantasy, anime, and science aficionados as well as all other varieties of geek can come together, we hope to facilitate the communication, collaboration, and recreation of our socially awkward minority and tap into the potential power of joint nerd-dom.
